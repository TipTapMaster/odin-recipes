Chicken Wings

Description

This is a classic recipe for crispy oven-baked buffalo wings.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon molasses

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Pat wings dry then toss with baking powder and salt. Arrange wings in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast wings until crispy, about 40 minutes. Flip wings halfway through roasting.
  4. While wings are baking, whisk hot sauce, butter, and molasses in large bowl.
  5. Remove wings from oven. If desired, adjust oven rack 6 inches from broiler and heat broiler. Broil wings for 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Add wings to sauce and toss to coat.
