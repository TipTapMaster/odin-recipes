Banana Bread

Description

This is a wonderful, easy to bake sweet quick bread. It has been a staple in my family for as long as I can remember.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 medium mashed ripe bananas
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1-2/3 cup all purpose unbleached flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease bottom of loaf pan.
  2. Combine sugar and butter in large bowl.
  3. Mic in eggs until well blended.
  4. Add bananas and water and blend together.
  5. Stir in remaining dry ingredients until moistened.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes. Cool 10 minutes.
  7. Loosen sides of loaf pan and remove from pan. Cool completely before slicing.
